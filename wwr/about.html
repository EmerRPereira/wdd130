History

In the heart of the mountains, Rafting Company Waterfall was born, a company founded by nature and adrenaline enthusiasts. It all began when a group of friends decided to transform their passion for rivers into an unforgettable experience for those seeking thrills and direct contact with nature.

Adventure Awaits You!

Our rafting trips are designed for all levels of experience, from beginners to seasoned adventurers. Our experienced guides will ensure your safety while providing an exciting and fun experience on the water. Whether you're looking for a family adventure or an adrenaline rush, we have the perfect trip for you.
